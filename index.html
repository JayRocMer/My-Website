Minecraft is for everyone!

Family members and friends of all ages will enjoy the game!

Create your world, build houses, milk cows, herd sheep, dig for diamonds, and more!


You'll start out without any gear but will have a chest full of tools and food, if you chose to do so before creating your World. After that, you'll want to build a shelter to protect everyone from angry mobs. During the day, attack chickens, sheep, cows, or pigs, to feed yourself and your family and keep your energy bar full!


Things to do:

    Chop wood

    Mine coal, iron, copper, diamonds, and emeralds

    Find hidden mineshafts and shipwrecks

    Brew potions of Night Vision, Fire Resistance, and Water Breathing

    Find farms to raid of their goods! Crops, chests, and job blocks are some of the best things to take from the villagers

Purposes of:

SAND

    Smelt it in the furnace to make glass

    Dig a moat around your house then fill it with water or lava

    Keep mobs buried until you're ready for them!

COBBLESTONE

    Smelt it in the furnace to make smooth stone - a gorgeous material for the flooring of your house.

Mobs

    Zombies

    Witches

    Pillagers

    Phantoms

    Spiders

    Skeletons

    Zombie Children

[Hint: you can heal zombie villagers!]
